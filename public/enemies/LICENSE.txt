--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONTRIBUTING CUSTOM ART
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If creating custom enemy art for this project:

1. License: Must be CC0 or compatible with open-source game
2. Size: 24x24 pixels (to match existing enemy grid)
3. Format: PNG with transparency
4. Style: Match Kenney pixel platformer aesthetic
5. Palette: Keep colors limited (8-16 colors max)
6. Animation: Provide 2+ frames for walk/idle cycles

Place new sprites in /public/enemies/ and update:
- /src/data/AssetManifest.ts - Add to enemies section
- /src/scenes/BootScene.ts - Add preload call
- Relevant enemy class in /src/objects/enemies/
